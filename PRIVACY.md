# Privacy Policy

**Keln — Workout Tracker**
Last updated: April 29, 2026

## Summary

Keln does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your workout data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

Keln collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No account or sign-up required
- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting
- No advertising
- No cookies
- No data shared with third parties

## Data Storage

All workout data (workouts, templates, and preferences) is stored locally on your device. Keln does not use cloud storage, servers, or any internet connection.

If you use the export feature, you choose where the file goes. Keln does not transmit it anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

Keln does not integrate with any third-party services.

## Your Rights (GDPR / EEA Users)

Keln does not collect or process personal data, so there is no personal data to access, correct, delete, or port. You are already in full control — your workout data lives only on your device and can be exported or deleted at any time through the app.

If you believe you have a data-related concern, contact us at spector.t103@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy

Keln does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
