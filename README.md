# Keln Support

Thanks for using Keln — a simple, private workout tracker.

## Get Help

If you're experiencing a bug, have a question, or want to suggest a feature:

1. **Check existing issues** — someone may have reported the same thing
2. **Open a new issue** — describe what happened, what you expected, and your iOS version

[Open an Issue](https://github.com/Stelios-T/keln-support/issues/new)

## Contact

## About Keln

Keln is free, fully offline, and doesn't collect any data. Your workouts stay on your device.
